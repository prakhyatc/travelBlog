Destinations

Austin

Austin, a city that captivated my heart in 2022. As I stood in awe of the magnificent State Capitol, its grandeur and rich history echoed through every corner. The vibrant energy of Austin's streets filled me with a sense of adventure, from live music pouring out of every venue to the mouthwatering flavors of local cuisine.

Denver

Denver, a city that left an indelible mark on my soul. In 2023, I found myself immersed in the breathtaking beauty of the Rocky Mountains as I explored the vibrant streets of Denver. From the picturesque views atop Red Rocks Amphitheatre to the awe-inspiring exhibits at the Denver Art Museum, every moment was a testament to the city's vibrant culture.

Oklahoma

Oklahoma, a hidden gem that captivated my heart. In my journey to Oklahoma, I discovered the enchanting beauty of Turner Falls, where nature's cascading waters embraced me with tranquility. The thrill of testing my luck at the lively casinos and the joy of savoring delectable dishes at charming local restaurants created unforgettable memories. Oklahoma's rich Native American heritage and warm hospitality added depth to my experience, making it a place I will always hold dear.
